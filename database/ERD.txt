Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD)

1. USERS
   - user_id (PK)
   - username
   - email
   - ... (other fields)

2. TYPES
   - type_id (PK)
   - type_name
   - ... (other fields)

3. POKEMON
   - pokemon_id (PK)
   - name
   - type_id (FK)
   - ... (other fields)

4. MOVES
   - move_id (PK)
   - move_name
   - type_id (FK)
   - ... (other fields)

5. ABILITIES
   - ability_id (PK)
   - ability_name
   - ... (other fields)

6. TEAMS
   - team_id (PK)
   - team_name
   - user_id (FK)
   - ... (other fields)

7. TEAM_MEMBERS
   - team_member_id (PK)
   - team_id (FK)
   - pokemon_id (FK)
   - ... (other fields)

8. BATTLES
   - battle_id (PK)
   - team_id (FK)
   - opponent_id (FK)
   - winner_id (FK)
   - ... (other fields)

Relationships:
- USERS can have multiple TEAMS (1 to Many)
- TEAMS can have multiple TEAM_MEMBERS (1 to Many)
- POKEMON can have multiple MOVES (1 to Many)
- POKEMON can have multiple ABILITIES (1 to Many)
- BATTLES involve two TEAMS (Many to Many)
